How To Use

We just took baby steps to make this project work and it is still in Alpha Phase. Meaning that most of the planned functionality is not here yet, and the app we have may still be buggy. Yet we belive in power of Open-Source community, thus we provide the software as is in hopes more and more people will get interested and help us grow.

To use it in current stage you should:

1

Download or clone repo

https://github.com/Gogolian/open-humanity
2

Launch index.html

no need to use local server, just open it in your browser
3

Answer Chatbot questions

As we grow, more and more questions and categories will be added
4

Download Results

'Save conversation history' to download it on your local computer and store it safely.
Optionally click Clear log to clear conversation from local storage.
About Open Humanity

Unfortunately, humans are mortal beings, and there is currently no way to live past the age of 100. While there may be advancements in longevity in the future, we must accept our mortality as an inevitable part of life. However, we still want to leave a legacy behind us. We create art, write books, and contribute to the world in many ways to make a lasting impact.

Our great-grandparents left us diaries, our grandparents left us photos, and our parents left us videos. Now, we have the opportunity to leave something else behind.

There are stories about people who have been turned into chatbots. With the current technology, it is possible to create a digital replica of a person. All we need is data.
