Our digital backup service allows you to create a comprehensive archive of your life in digital form.

Our Mission

At Open Humanity, we believe that everyone should have the ability to make a digital backup of themselves.

By doing so, you can preserve your memories, your personality, and your values for future generations to come.

You can store your most cherished memories, such as photos and videos, as well as important documents, like your resume or medical records.

By making a digital backup of yourself, you ensure that your personal history is preserved and can be shared with future generations.

Our easy-to-use tools and resources make the process simple and accessible. With our easy-to-use tools and resources, you can get started today and ensure that your legacy lives on.
